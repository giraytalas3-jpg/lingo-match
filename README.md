# Lingo Match

Lingo Match is a hobby-focused language matching platform designed to connect language learners through shared interests.

## Features

- **Language Selection**: Choose the languages you want to learn or practice.
- **Questionnaire-Based Matching**: Find the perfect language partner based on a detailed matching algorithm.
- **AI-Supported Chat Coaching**: Get real-time help and tips from an AI coach during your conversations.
- **Location-Free Design**: Connect with partners from all over the world without geographical restrictions.
- **Hobby Focus**: Match with people who share your interests, making conversations more engaging and natural.
- **AI Conversation Bots**: Practice your skills anytime with our advanced AI bots.
